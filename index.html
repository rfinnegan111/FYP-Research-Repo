Investigating Ethical AI: Capabilities & Implications in the Cybersecurity Space



Project Description

This paper analyses the Artificial Intelligence state of art and the impact on cybersecurity. It examines developments within the field and the associated security impacts. Furthermore, the paper covers AI Ethics, scoping the characteristics of an Ethical AI alongside risks and concerns. The paper identifies measures that may be used to address these ethical concerns to yield a positive outcome.

To address the research questions posed the paper employs two methods of qualitative research those being a content analysis and a case study analysis from an end user perspective.

The paper contributes to an understanding of the security landscape within AI enhanced spaces. Providing guidance to organisations seeking a strategy to implement Ethical AI in cybersecurity space.
